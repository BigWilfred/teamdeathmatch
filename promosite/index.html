  • The Problem

    Despite a huge influx of mobile users reading news daily on their phones, often during their daily commute on public transport, people are still required to scroll through news websites and apps to find what they want to read. Opening up news stories one by one is a time consuming and annoying process when you’re trying to find something interesting to read. Some users are inconvenienced by the nature of the design on these sites, for example, sports news it typically featured only at the very bottom of the news app’s front page.



    Though such readily available mobile news has been a great push in the right direction, these apps are as impersonal as a newspaper. They display what they think you’ll want to read, but really you probably tend to read the same sections and look for the same types of articles as you normally do. Which is where Bulletin comes in.

  • The Technology

    With smartphones becoming nearly ubiquitous within our society, news is being increasingly targeted towards mobile users both in the form of responsive websites and mobile applications.



    Bulletin is currently releasing for both Android and iOS devices and will be available on the relevant app stores. Delivering your personalised news right to your phone will enable you to access the content you want when you want it. Bulletin utilises all the features of a mobile application, including push notifications, easy sharing options, and opportunities to connect with your friends and contacts.

  • The Audience

    Bulletin is the perfect application for daily commuters who already use their time to check up on the latest news. Being able to select a specific amount of time means you can get the perfectly curated list of videos delivered straight to your phone to match your preferences and your commute time.



    However, Bulletin can be used by anyone at any time, not just commuters! We aggregate and display news from a number of different media outlets, so we’re guaranteed to have content anyone would be interested in. Setting up preferences for media outlets and category preferences will allow you to customise your experience even further.

  • The Solution

    Bulletin is an app that aims to solve a problem. It has been designed specifically to cater to the issues with the current state of mobile news that have been outlined above, and seeks to provide people with an alternative way to consume news that’s all about them rather than about the big media outlets.



    Pushing forward in this direction, Bulletin is set to revolutionise the space of mobile news. With upcoming updates and features already scheduled to be developed in the future, now is the time to test out Bulletin and see how it could improve your commute or news experience!
